Introduction to EPSILON: Who Are We?

EPSILON is an event that aims to foster creativity and innovation among all it's participants. We aim to motivate and provide an open platform to youth who are skilled in exra-curricular activities like art, creative writing, and speaking so that they can unleash their creativity. Believing that extra-curricular activities are very much necessary for the overall development of an individual, we encourage students to participate in the events we conduct.
We believe that everyone has an inner Picasso or a Shakespeare and we hope to help everyone discover their inner talents.
